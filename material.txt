Application and documentation: The borrower submits a loan application and provides the necessary documentation, such as income verification and proof of assets.

Credit check: The bank conducts a credit check to determine the borrower's creditworthiness.

Property appraisal: An appraiser is sent to the property to determine its value.

Underwriting: The bank's underwriting department reviews the loan application, credit report, and property appraisal to determine if the loan meets the bank's guidelines.

Approval or denial: The bank makes a decision to approve or deny the loan application.

Closing: If the loan is approved, the borrower and the bank will complete the necessary paperwork and the loan funds will be disbursed.
